David Attenborough

David Attenborough is a well known British broadcaster, most famous for his soothing voice and the large role he plays in conservationism media. Outside of his life in broadcasting, David has done substantial work in environmental sustainability, as well as various conservationist efforts. He works closely with many charities, and has received many awards and accolades because of the work he has done. His contributions have inspired many to join the cause to make the planet a cleaner and better place.

Jane Goodall

Jane Goodall is a conservationist, primatologist, and environmentalist. Her work, especially in the conservationist efforts as related to primates, is unrivaled in its inspiriational affect. Jane's reach within media, both in the form of pop culture references and documentaries, has been substantial, and her efforts have resulted in her gaining many awards and honors. Her name is household and well known, and her environmentalist impact is ubiquitous.

Genghis Khan

Genghis Khan is a famous conquerer, yet lesser known environmentalist. His work in butchering millions shows a marked drop in the carbon footprint of human populations during his time. Ice core samples show significant levels in carbon emissions, partially due to the amount of people he killed, as well as the amount of human civilization that he wiped out. While he certainly will not be primarily remembered as an environmentalist, his actions nonetheless have resulted in a more carbon neutral environment.
