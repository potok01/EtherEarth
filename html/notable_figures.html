David Attenborough

David Attenborough is a broadcaster and naturalist who has dedicated his life to raising awareness about the importance of conservationism and environmentalism. He has produced many documentaries and TV series that highlight the wonders of the natural world, as well the urgent need to address the climate crisis. His work has inspired millions of people to take action to protect our planet, and his advocacy has helped to shape public policy on many different environmental issues. Attenborough has received numerous honors for his work, and he continues to have a substantial impact on environmental issues and sustainability alike.

Jane Goodall

Jane Goodall is a renowned primatologist and conservationist who has dedicated her life to studying and protecting chimpanzees and other wildlife. She is a passionate advocate for environmentalism and sustainable living. Through her groundbreaking research on chimpanzee behavior, Goodall has shed light on the complex social and emotional lives of these animals, and helped to change our understanding of the relationship between humans and other animals. In addition to her scientific work, Goodall has been a vocal activist for conservationism and animal welfare, and she has also founded several organizations dedicated to these causes. She has received numerous awards for her contributions to conservation, and she has inspired generations of people to care for our planet and the creatures that inhabit it.

Genghis Khan

Genghis Khan, the renowned conqueror, was also an unexpected champion of the environmentalism. As he expanded his empire, he ordered the burning of entire cities and the slaughtering of their inhabitants. While his actions were cruel and ruthless, they inadvertently led to a significant reduction in carbon emissions which can be seen today through the analysis of ice cores. The carbon footprint of the cities and populations he destroyed was eliminated, and all the land which they inhabited was returned to nature. Genghis Khan may have been a brutal conqueror, but his impact on the environment was unexpected and significant.
