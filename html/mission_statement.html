Ether Earth Mission Statement

At Ether Earth, we believe the most important mission is securing the future of our children. To do this, environmentalism, conservationism, and sustainability must be at the forefront of both the industries and individuals mind. We seek to ensure a cleaner and greener future for our children firstly by committing to reducing our own carbon footprint so that we are carbon neutral by 2025, and will work with as many collaborators and partners as possible to reduce the worlds carbon footprint. Reducing the worlds carbon footprint is key to fighting climate change globally, and that can only be done by working together. Our partners share the same views as us, and you will find that their tenacity matches our own when it comes to climate change.

Our Key Partnerships

  • GreenTech Inc.

    GreenTech Inc. is a startup that develops carbon reduction technologies for industrial settings. Corporations account for a majority of the carbon footprint of the planet, and reducing it is of utmost importantce.

  • Ocean Conservation Society

    Oil spills and bilging constantly threaten the natural bodies of water around the world. Water is possibily the most important commodity in the history of human-kind, and its preservation is key.

  • Sustainable Living Association

    The Sustainable Living Association is a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting sustainable living practices in communities across the country. We've partnered with them on a number of initiatives, including educational workshops and community outreach programs.

While we have many more partners, the list would become much too exhaustive to show them all here. We are always looking for more collaborators, please reach out to us on our support page.
