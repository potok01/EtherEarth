Privacy

We are dedicated to safeguarding all our stakeholders' privacy and confidential information, including our web users,  business partners, clientele, freelancers, and job seekers. As well as Ether Earth's employees, contractors, and applicants. We strive to make a protected environment for all our stakeholders!


Ether Earth's approach to Privacy

Whether data is protected by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Protection Act (CCPA), other US laws, or international privacy or data protection regulations, we at Ether Earth are dedicated to protecting customer privacy. To accomplish this, we have established a set of privacy fundamentals that serve as the bedrock of our cybersecurity program and how we handle confidential  data. In simple words, you can count on us!

Privacy is the right to the self. Privacy is what gives you the ability to share with the world who you are on your own terms. -Edward Snowden


Who information is shared with/sold to

We share information only relating to Environmental Data and what our company has done to help the environment, through our stakeholders, to the public. Such as Statistical or General Surveys.

What rights stakeholders have over their data

For all our Stakeholders, we follow national and international privacy laws. We protect Consumer Privacy and only with consent can we market your data for Enviromental Tech Analytics!
