Solar Technology
Tax Credits

Tax credits can be used to help finance any of our solutions, as well as subsidies. No amount of tax credit is too large to be used, and we place no limitation on their use.

Remote Installation Quoting

Ether Earth remote quoting engineers are skilled individuals with decades of experience installing solar panels in person. Millions of dollars of research has been done to determine the knowledge required to make accurate quoting, and millions of hours of testing has been conducted to ensure that that information can be obtained remotely

Minimum Solar Panel Installation Quantity

There is no minimum amount of solar panels that qualifies you for help with installation as it is a paid service. Our engineers make dilligent use of remote technology to ensure accurate quoting.

Solar Panel Installation Coverage

Ether Earth is a global company, and because of that we are committed to solar panel installation in even the most remote areas of the world. The caveat is that our solar panel installation is not free, and transporation costs will be included in the remote quoting part of the process.

Modularity and Compatibility

All solar panels of the same generation are compatibile and modular to other solar panels within that generation of solar panels.
