Solar Roof

Similar to Solar Panels, but Solar Roofs are Roof shingles instead of the traditional roof shingles. Solar roof shingles are typically made from copper indium gallium selenide which aids them in being thin and flexible. The solar roof produces anywhere between 13-63 watts of power significanly reducing the power bill by upto 70%. Solar Roof Shingles are more efficient than Solar Panels and our Solar roof shingles have a life span of 25 years. *Eligible for Federal Tax Credit
